Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

What we collect and why

When you create an account, we collect your email address and password hash (bcrypt, 12 rounds — we never store your plain password). We collect session data (session ID, IP address, user agent) to keep you logged in and detect abuse. We log actions you take (which papers you analyze, pruning operations) for 90 days.

Third-party processors

  • Neon.tech — PostgreSQL database
  • Cloudflare R2 — object storage for graphs and exports
  • Koyeb — backend hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing (we never see your card number)
  • Resend — transactional email
  • Groq — LLM API (paper abstracts only — never personal data)
  • hCaptcha — bot protection on login and registration

Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, and objection. Exercise these from your account settings or email privacy@arivu.app. Account deletion requests are processed within 24 hours.

Cookies

We use one cookie: arivu_session — strictly necessary for keeping you logged in. HTTP-only, Secure, SameSite=Lax. No tracking cookies. No advertising cookies.

Data controller: Dev (Arivu). Contact: privacy@arivu.app
